STORE YOUR LIFE ETERNALLY



No one can predict the future, but anyone can prepare for the future. Your photos, videos, documents, everything you leave behind is irreplaceable for your family and friends. Upload and store precious memories to a secure place while you are alive. Those memories continue to live on Memorial Paradise.

Create, Own and Explore Your Ideal Dream World



In the game, you can create your dream world, own land and items, upload your life and publish it to people you selected. It's like building your own memorial museum. This way your relatives and friends can visit you at any time. Imagine if you could visit your ancestors and see their lives. What did they look like? What did they do? We are all part of history. Small daily decisions overlap and lead to your present and future. Your very existence is a miracle, just as you may not be here if your ancestors made different decisions.

MAKE PROFITS



In Memorial Paradise, you can make profits from trading land, items, MEPA tokens, renting land and holding events. If you become a MPD (Memorial Paradise Designer) or MPC (Memorial Paradise Company), you can create assets in the game, list them on NFT markets, sell services in the game, and make profits.
